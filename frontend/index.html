Important Information

Maximal dosages:

  • Adults: up to 80mg per day
  • Children and Adolescents: up to 40mg per day

Things to avoid while taking Medikinet:

  • Consuming alcohol
  • Driving or operating heavy machinery until you know how Medikinet affects you
  • Taking other medications that may interact with Medikinet without consulting a healthcare professional

Warnings:

  • Consult your doctor before using Medikinet if you have a history of heart problems, mental health issues, or substance abuse.
  • Medikinet may not be suitable for individuals with certain medical conditions or who are taking certain medications. Consult your healthcare provider for personalized advice.
